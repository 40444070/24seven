About Me

Who am I?

My name is Marton Varga a videographer graduate who is looking for opportunities for photo/video work so please contact me for quality but cheap work. I’ve started 24SevenVisuals to collect and showcase my work and to use as a gateway to turn my passion into a business. I’m looking to create amazing stories with beautiful shots and to show businesses how powerful a short film can be.

What do I do?

I’m currently a Web Design and Development student in Edinburgh looking. I’m trying to connect the dots in-between my previous experience in videography and photography and the profession of designing content for the web and become a digital media expert.

Background

I’ve always been interested in technology and fixing gadgets as a kid, therefore, I went to study IT in High School. During my student years, I started filming extreme sports videos and bought my first Camera and documented basically everything. At the time my parents would tell me to put that camera down for a second but now they are quite thankful for all those memories being kept.

Creating visual content has gradually become my passion, therefore, I went to study Cinematography and Animation to Budapest where I graduated in 2015. There I picked up lots of extra skills regarding both the filmmaking process and the documentation around it but have learned that the most important part in a film or behind photos is the story.

After graduation, I have worked on a couple of video projects including weddings and films for events but I decided to move to Scotland looking for new experiences.
